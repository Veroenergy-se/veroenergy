About Vero

The Story Behind the Perfect Bar

We're not just another energy bar company. We're two friends from Sweden who got tired of choosing between taste and performance, between clean ingredients and actual results.

Our Story

The Problem We Faced

Picture this: It's 2 AM, you're deep in a study session or working on that project that could change everything. You need energy, but coffee makes you jittery, energy drinks crash you harder than they lift you, and protein bars taste like cardboard.

Two Years of Testing

Hundreds of combinations, working with nutritionists, refusing to compromise

Our Solution

So we did what any obsessive perfectionists would do: we spent two years in the kitchen, testing hundreds of combinations, working with nutritionists, and refusing to compromise.

We discovered that salted caramel wasn't just delicious – when done right, it was the perfect vehicle for clean caffeine, plant protein, and zero added sugar. One flavor, perfected.

Why We Do This

We believe that focus is a superpower. In a world designed to scatter your attention, the ability to concentrate deeply is what separates dreamers from achievers.

Every Vero bar is crafted for the ambitious minds who refuse to settle. Students, entrepreneurs, creators – anyone who knows that great work requires great fuel.

What We Stand For

Clean Ingredients

100% plant-based, zero added sugar, no artificial nonsense. We believe your body deserves real ingredients that work in harmony with your natural energy systems.

Functional Performance

Every ingredient has a purpose. Green tea caffeine for clean energy, L-theanine for calm focus, plant protein for sustained performance. No fillers, no shortcuts.

Relentless Quality

We could have launched with "good enough." Instead, we spent two years perfecting one flavor because we believe excellence isn't negotiable.

Meet the Team

We're two friends from Stockholm who turned our frustration with mediocre energy products into a mission to create something better.
