Our Story

Three Brothers. One Mission.

We're from Stockholm. Between school, sports, and building side projects, we were constantly on the move. Every energy bar, drink, or snack seemed to compromise on something important—taste, performance, or health.

The Journey Begins

Two years in the making. Countless late nights perfecting the formula. We tested every ingredient, every combination, until we created something that actually works—plant-based, zero added sugar, clean energy.

Built For Ambitious Minds

CAFO isn't just another energy bar. It's fuel for people who refuse to slow down. No crash, no jitters, no guilt. Just clean energy that keeps up with your ambitions. This is just the beginning.
